Core features
Tokenization
Tokenization forms the foundation of our chatbot's language comprehension. It involves breaking down user input into individual tokens or words. By segmenting sentences into words, our chatbot gains a better understanding of user queries. This critical NLP technique paves the way for accurate processing and relevant responses.
Lemmatization & Stemming
To enhance the efficiency of language understanding, our chatbot employs lemmatization. This process reduces words to their base or root form, eliminating inflections and variations. Similar to lemmatization, Stemming involves removing prefixes or suffixes from words to obtain their root form.
Bag of Words (BoW)
Bag of Words is a fundamental technique that enables our chatbot to understand the context of user queries. It creates a "bag" of all unique words from the user's input, disregarding grammar and word order. This statistical approach allows our chatbot to focus on word occurrence, aiding in context-based responses.
